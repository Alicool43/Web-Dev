Pancakes

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1
Sugar 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Heat a pan.
  3. Pour the batter into the pan.
  4. Cook until golden brown.
